About SeatSnap

SeatSnap is a simple web application designed to help you remember and schedule flight check-ins for domestic airlines.

Remind

SeatSnap's Remind feature is simple. Plug in your flight data and we'll send an SMS and email one hour before your airline allows you to check-in to your flight.

Both messages will contain a link to your airline's check-in page, allowing quick and easy reservations. Say goodbye to C seats!

Schedule

SeatSnap's Schedule feature takes the hassle out of check-ins by having someone check-in for you, right on time. Think of it as a concierge service for frequent flyers.

Simply plug in your flight data, pay $5 to get your own flight check-in assistant, and you're set. We'll email you with info on when we checked in, your queue number, and more depending on the airline. It's easy!
